Welcome to Filmdatify, a fun and innovative web application that connects users to others who have similar tastes in entertainment. Using the IMDB dataset, Filmdatify predicts which users will be compatible based upon genre, actor, and director information. There are a countless number of potential user groups: students, movie aficionados, young professionals, researchers, lonely single adults, and even couples searching for new friends with shared interests will be excited by the prospect of finding people who love the entertainment that they enjoy.
